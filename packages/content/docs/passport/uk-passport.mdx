## Validation Rules

**Regex:** `/^[A-Z0-9]{9}$/`

**Format:**
- Exactly 9 characters
- Alphanumeric characters (A-Z, 0-9)
- Covers current and older formats

**Valid examples:** 123456789, AB1234567, A12345678
