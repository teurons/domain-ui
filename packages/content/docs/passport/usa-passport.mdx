## Validation Rules

**Regex:** `/^(?:[A-Z][0-9]{8}|[0-9]{9}|[A-Z][0-9]{7})$/`

**Format:**
- Current format: 1 letter + 8 digits (9 total)
- Older format: 9 digits only
- Rare older format: 1 letter + 7 digits (8 total)

**Valid examples:** A12345678, 123456789, X1234567
