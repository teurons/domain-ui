## Validation Rules

**Regex:** `/^[A-Z]?[0-9]{7,9}$/`

**Format:**
- Optional letter (A-Z) at the start
- 7 to 9 digits (0-9)
- Covers current, older, and rare formats

**Valid examples:** A12345678, 123456789, X1234567
